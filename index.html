About Us

We help kids in the Philippines who are living in poverty and cannot afford to buy food. Our mission is to provide nutritious meals and support to children who need it the most, ensuring they have the energy and health to thrive.

At HelpDHungry, we believe that every child deserves a chance to grow up healthy and happy. We work closely with local communities and volunteers to distribute food and raise awareness about child hunger and poverty.

Donations we receive go directly towards purchasing food supplies, organizing feeding programs, and supporting sustainable initiatives that empower families to improve their quality of life.

If you want to make a difference, you can join us by donating, volunteering, or spreading the word about our cause. Together, we can help build a brighter future for these children.
